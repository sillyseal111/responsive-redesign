Sky Views Planetarium Show
Starts: 2PM Saturdays and Sundays

Important Information:
  • Everyone attending the show must have a ticket.
  • Tickets must be purchased online.
  • Planetarium show tickets are free every first Saturday of the month for City of Providence residents.

  • Children must be age 4 and older to attend the show.
  • Children age 15 and under must be accompanied by an adult.
  • Please plan to arrive at least 15 minutes prior to the show start time to check-in for the Planetarium show.
